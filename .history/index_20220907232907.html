About Me Hi, I'm Justin and thank you for your interest! This website showcases my skillset, projects, and my hobby with custom keyboards!

Get to know me!

My name is Justin Nguyen and I'm an Electical & Software Engineer located in Calgary, Alberta. I finished my B.Sc in Electrical Engineering, and I'm now pursuing a M.Eng in Software Engineering with an expected graduation date of April 2023.

During my internship I worked at NOVA Chemicals as a project manager for Instrument & Control and Electrical projects. I've recently completed my externship at MakeSens Inc. where I worked as a PCB designer and software developer. I designed a new sensor board and helped develop the Android app.

I love creating and designing microcontroller projects as a hobby, and I was able to apply that knowledge and passion to my work experience. I've also expanded this hobby towards custom mechanical keyboards, which I love to build and share! I aspire to create and design my own custom keyboard soon!

Fun fact, this website is hosted on my Raspberry Pi 4 Model B. You can view the steps I've gone through on my Github repo along with guides for many other applications!
